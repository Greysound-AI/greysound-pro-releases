## Greysound Pro 0.1.12

- Fixed corrupted desktop playback on Windows, including noise that could continue after stopping transport. Secondary MIDI-preview audio now correctly initialises reused device buffers.
- Fixed Bounce Mix deadlocks during normal completion on Windows and during mid-bounce cancellation on all desktop platforms.
- Fixed offline bouncing for MIDI-only sessions that use Greysound's built-in Synth, Drum Machine, Instrument Sampler, or Drum Sampler.
